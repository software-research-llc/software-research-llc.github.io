--- title: Software Research LLC ---

A few of our more impressive projects

European Food Safety Authority

Natural Language Recognition

Implemented an end-to-end natural language processing package for the European Food Safety Authority in the domains of animal and plant research journals: machine learning models and software were developed to enhance the content information available during meta-analyses and systematic reviews.

Result: still under evaluation.

National Institute of Justice

Real Time Crime Forecasting Challenge

Developed improved statistical and machine learning algorithms to assist law enforcement in location based crime forecasting, which is a method used by law enforcement agencies around the world to allocate on-call law enforcement officers efficiently.

Result: we won.

Office of the Under Secretary of Defense for Intelligence

ODNI-OUSD(I) Xamine Challenge

Designed a system for the Office of the Under Secretary of Defense for Intelligence to perform machine verification of collected information (i.e. automated provenance checking).

Result: we won.

Office of the Director of National Intelligence

ODNI-OUSD(I) Xtend Challenge

Designed a system for the Office of the Director of National Intelligence to perform automated evaluation of analytic products (intelligence community reports), which is currently performed manually by the ODNI AIS (Analytic Integrity and Standards) staff.

Result: we won.

Department of Homeland Security

DHS / TSA Passenger Screening Algorithm Challenge

Implemented software for use with "millimeter wave" scanners, as used by the Department of Homeland Security; relies on several deep neural net architectures and the DHS hardware units to automate the process of scanning subjects (e.g. airport passengers).

Result: top 9% of participants.

Department of Health and Human Services

Statistical Software for Healthcare Oversight Challenge

Rewrote RAT-STATS, a software application used by the Department of Health and Human Services’ Office of Inspector General’s Office of Audit Services division. RAT-STATS was commissioned in the late 1970s, and served as the gold standard tool for their division ever since.

Result: paid finalist.
